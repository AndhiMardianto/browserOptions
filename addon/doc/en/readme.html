Browser Options

Author : Andhi Mardianto

Introduction

Sometimes, we need to browse or visit websites using different browsers. This simple addon detects popular browsers installed on your device, such as Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.

Press NVDA+O to display the search dialog. Enter the URL or value you want to visit and press Enter or also can pres tab to button search A dialog will appear allowing you to choose from the browsers installed on your device. After selecting the desired browser, press Enter, and the program will close. Enjoy your browsing experience!

In release 1.1.0, the favorite feature is available. You can open it from the NVDA+SHIFT+O command.

You can also change the shortcut in the NVDA Preferences under Input Gestures.
